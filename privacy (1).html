Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how publicholiday.today ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use publicholiday.today. However, the following information may be collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, browser type, and device type via Google Analytics
  • Approximate location — we use your IP address to detect your country automatically so we can show you relevant holiday information. We do not store your IP address.
  • Cookies — we use cookies to remember your country preference so you don't have to select it every visit

How We Use Your Information

  • To show you public holiday information relevant to your country
  • To remember your country preference between visits
  • To understand how people use our site so we can improve it
  • To serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also opt out at aboutads.info.

Third Party Services

We use the following third party services:

  • Nager.Date API — provides public holiday data. No personal data is sent to this service.
  • ipapi.co — used for country detection from your IP address. Your IP is not stored by us.
  • Google AdSense — displays advertisements. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — tracks anonymous usage statistics.

Cookies

We use a single cookie to store your country preference locally. This cookie does not contain any personally identifiable information and is stored only on your device.

Google AdSense may set additional cookies for advertising purposes. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Country preferences are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we do not store personal data on our servers, there is nothing to access or delete beyond what is stored locally in your browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
